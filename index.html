Our Journey, Your Beauty
Founded in 2019 by Patricia Reindelle Francisco, a proud Fernandina and licensed hair and beauty expert, Patricia Rein Style Studio started with a dream: to give every client the confidence that comes with feeling beautiful.

Mission: To provide high-quality hair, skin, and beauty services that enhance our clients’ natural features while promoting self-love and confidence.
Vision: To become a leading beauty and wellness hub in Pampanga — known for professionalism, style, and care.
Values:
  • 💖 Integrity in Service
  • ✂️ Creativity & Passion
  • 🌟 Personalized Attention
  • 🇵🇭 Local Pride – Proudly Kapampangan-run
We believe in the power of transformation — not just in looks, but in how you feel about yourself.
